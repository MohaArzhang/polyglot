About Us

Polyglot was founded by Roger Flambard in france, 1954. Roger had a little brother who was mentally incapacitated. His brother would try to teach small lizards to speak french and was devastated by their lack of responsiveness. Deeply moved, Roger decided to found polizardglot. This lizard teaching buisness was very unsuccesful due to lack of clientelle. Roger's son Samuel Flambard took over the buisness after Roger's death and changed it's target clientelle to humans and it's name to Polyglot.

At Polyglot, we offer a unique learning experience and we guarantee that learning a language will never be easier elsewhere. We believe that it is unfair that a student must bend to the rules of a language. When working with Polyglot , YOU decide how the language works. Tell that language who's boss.
